# Man-at-Arms

## Class Summary

The **Man-at-Arms** is a hardened front-rank martial combatant who overwhelms foes through weapon mastery, relentless attacks, and brutal finishing pressure.

### Core Identity
- **Primary Attributes:** Strength / Constitution
- **Level 10 Title:** Warlord
- **Hit Die:** d10
- **Armor:** Any armor
- **Shield:** Can equip shield
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** All weapons
- **Spellcasting:** None
- **Special Role:** Front-rank bruiser, weapon specialist, crit-focused melee attacker, sustained combat pressure class

## Features

### Improved Critical
All of your attacks score a **critical hit on 19-20**.

---

### Lithe Movement
While wearing **Light armor**, you roll **initiative with advantage**.

---

### Dual Wield
You may attack with **two melee weapons**, provided the off-hand weapon is **lighter** than your main-hand weapon.

When you attack with your main hand, you may make:
- **one additional off-hand attack** as a **bonus action**

---

### Weapon Specialization
Choose a single **weapon type**.

When attacking with that weapon, you gain:
- **+1 to hit**
- **+2 to damage**

---

### Uncapped Attribute Growth
You can increase:
- one attribute by **2**
- or two attributes by **1** each

---

### Retaliate
When you are hit by a **melee attack**, you may immediately make a melee attack against the attacker, provided:
- you are wielding a **melee weapon**
- the target is within your **reach**

Rules:
- this effect can occur only **once per turn**

---

### Extra Attack
When you take the **Attack** action, you may attack multiple times.

### Extra Attack Progression
- Level 5: **2 attacks**
- Level 9: **3 attacks**

---

### Frenzy
You may enter a **frenzied state**.

While frenzied:
- your melee attacks are made with **advantage**
- attack rolls against you are also made with **advantage**
- your melee attacks deal **+2 damage**

Rules:
- this state lasts until the **end of combat**
- once you enter Frenzy, you cannot voluntarily end it before combat ends

---

### Smell Blood
You deal **one additional damage die** on all hits against targets below **50% of their maximum hit points**.

---

## Notes

### Current Role
The Man-at-Arms is currently defined as:
- front-rank martial class
- flexible weapon user
- crit-focused sustained attacker
- retaliation-based melee bruiser
- execution and pressure specialist against wounded targets

### Current Open Mechanical Questions
These may need later clarification:
- how **Frenzy** is activated in action-economy terms and whether it has any use limit beyond its combat duration
- whether **Weapon Specialization** is permanent once chosen or may ever be changed later
- how **Dual Wield** interacts with **Extra Attack** at higher levels in exact attack sequencing terms
- whether **Smell Blood** applies to every successful hit in a multi-attack turn, including off-hand attacks and retaliation attacks
- how **Retaliate** is surfaced in the no-reaction combat model beyond its explicit once-per-turn trigger
