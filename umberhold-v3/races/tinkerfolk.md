# Tinkerfolk

## Overview
- **Size:** Small
- **Stat Bonuses:** +1 INT, +1 LCK

## Racial Perk
**Arcane Tinkering** — When a Tinkerfolk rolls a natural 20 with a damaging magic item, the critical effect is doubled, reflecting their innate attunement to magical devices.
