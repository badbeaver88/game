# Hillkin

## Overview
- **Size:** Large
- **Stat Bonuses:** +1 CON, +1 STR

## Racial Perk
**Towering Frame** — Hillkin have a natural Base AC of 11 due to their thick hide, but suffer -4 to Agility saving throws due to their large frame.
