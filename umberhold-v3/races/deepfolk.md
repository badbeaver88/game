# Deepfolk

## Overview
- **Size:** Medium
- **Stat Bonuses:** +1 CON, +1 STR

## Racial Perk
**Endurance & Poison Resilience** — Deepfolk gain +1 HP per level and a bonus to poison saving throws, reflecting their hardy constitution forged in underground environments.
