# Everkin

## Overview
- **Size:** Medium
- **Stat Bonuses:** +1 AGI, +1 INT

## Racial Perk
**Bow Mastery & Enchantment Resistance** — Everkin gain improved accuracy and critical hit chance with bows, and have innate resistance to sleep and charm effects.
