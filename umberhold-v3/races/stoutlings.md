# Stoutlings

## Overview
- **Size:** Small
- **Stat Bonuses:** +1 LCK, +1 AGI

## Racial Perk
**Lucky & Nimble** — Stoutlings gain +4 to lockpicking checks and +2 to all saving throws, reflecting their quick reflexes and uncanny fortune.
