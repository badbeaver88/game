# Umberhold Experience Canon

This document captures the current approved rules for:
- monster XP values by monster level
- XP distribution to the party
- level-up access at inns

It does **not** modify `DESIGN.md`.

## 2. XP Distribution

Experience gained by the party is:
- awarded when monsters are slain
- shared equally among **surviving party members**

Characters who are not surviving members at the end of the encounter do **not** receive a share of that encounter's experience.

---

## 3. Leveling Up

When a character has enough experience to advance to the next level:
- they must go to the nearest **inn**
- they choose the option to **advance**

### Level-Up Cost
- there is **no gold cost** to level up

### Note
The full level-up rules are still to be detailed separately.

## 4. Approved Rules Captured Here

This document locks in the following:
- monsters award XP based on monster level
- the XP table for monster levels 1-20 is defined
- XP is shared equally among surviving party members
- leveling up is performed at an inn
- leveling up has no gold cost
- detailed level-up rules will be defined separately
