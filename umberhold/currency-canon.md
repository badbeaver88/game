# Umberhold Currency Canon

This document captures the current approved currency system for Umberhold.

It does **not** modify `MASTER.md`.

---

## Coin Types

There are currently **three coins** in Umberhold:

| Coin Type | Value |
|---|---|
| **Silver Rook** | Base coin |
| **Gold Crown** | 10 Silver Rooks |
| **Platinum Stag** | 25 Gold Crowns |

---

## Coin Conversion

### Base Conversion
- **1 Gold Crown** = **10 Silver Rooks**
- **1 Platinum Stag** = **25 Gold Crowns**

### Expanded Conversion
- **1 Platinum Stag** = **250 Silver Rooks**

---

## Notes

- The **Silver Rook** is the standard everyday coin.
- The **Gold Crown** is the main higher-value trade coin.
- The **Platinum Stag** is used primarily by the wealthy.

## Still Open for Later Canonization

The following are still open and may need later clarification:

- whether very small prices use fractional Silver Rooks or are rounded
- whether there are copper-equivalent sub-coins or not
- how shops display mixed-currency totals in the UI
