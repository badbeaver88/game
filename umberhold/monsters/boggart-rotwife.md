## Notes

### Rare Drop
In addition to its normal loot, a Boggart Rotwife has a chance to drop:
- **Silver Unholy Symbol** — 5%

### Silver Unholy Symbol Summary
A crude, tarnished icon etched with profane markings, sticky with old grime.
- mundane
- worth 25 gp

### Spell Note
**Rotwife’s Lullaby** affects one enemy and up to one adjacent ally on each side in the same rank, for a maximum of 3 targets. Sleeping targets may repeat the save at the end of each of their turns, and the effect ends immediately if a target takes damage.
