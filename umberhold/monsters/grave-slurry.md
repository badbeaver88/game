## Notes

### Item Destruction Note
A successful **Pseudopod** hit generates a **MINOR ITEM DESTRUCTION** check with a **DC of 5**.

### Encounter Note
Grave Slurries drag themselves through crypts, pits, and churned burial earth, fouling the ground as they advance.
