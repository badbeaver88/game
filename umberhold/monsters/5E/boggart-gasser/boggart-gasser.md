## Notes

### Rare Drop
In addition to its normal loot, a Boggart Gasser has a chance to drop:
- **Stoppered Gas Vial** — 10%

### Stoppered Gas Vial Summary
A cloudy glass vial stoppered with black wax, still swirling with a foul green vapor that stings the eyes.
- non-magical
- worth 30 gp

### Tactics Note
Boggart Gassers prefer to fight from the rear ranks, hurling poison into weakened or tightly packed enemies while larger boggarts keep victims pinned in place.
