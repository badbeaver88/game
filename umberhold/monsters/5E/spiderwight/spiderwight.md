## Notes

### Rare Drop
In addition to its normal loot, a Spiderwight has a chance to contain:
- **Gravebreaker Mace** — 1%

### Graveflame Note
**Wall of Graveflame** targets at **range 3**.
- targets must make a **DC 14 Agility save**
- on a failure, the target takes **3d8 unholy damage**
- on a success, the target takes half damage
- if the save fails by **5 or more**, the target becomes **Frightened** until the end of its next turn
- **Recharge 6**
