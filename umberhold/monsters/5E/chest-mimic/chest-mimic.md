# Chest Mimic

## Stat Block

| Category | Details |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Chest Mimic |
| **Description** | A ravenous, shape-shifting horror that lurks in the guise of a wooden chest. Its lid often sits slightly ajar, a glint of coin within to bait the greedy. Up close, the illusion falters—grain becomes flesh, hinges twitch, and the interior is lined with rows of grinding teeth. Its saliva carries a paralysing toxin, used to incapacitate prey before it is consumed. When it strikes, the chest snaps shut with violent hunger. Occasionally, a pair of gloves can be found within—remains of a thief who reached in and never withdrew their hands. |

## Notes

### Rare Drop
In addition to its normal loot, a Chest Mimic has a chance to drop:
- **Gloves of the Black Hand** — 5%

### Paralysis Note
A creature hit by the Chest Mimic’s **Bite** must make a **DC 13 Constitution save** or become **Paralyzed**.
- the target may repeat the save at the end of each of its turns
- the effect ends on a success
