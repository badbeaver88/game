| **Description** | A gaunt, corpse-pale figure wrapped in rotting burial cloths, its hollow eyes burning with a cold, dim light. Its movements are slow but purposeful, each step heavy with the weight of the grave. These are the dead of nobles and royals, buried with precious things to take into the afterlife—riches that draw the greedy and desperate. Their tombs are often targeted by grave robbers, and those who disturb them awaken something cold and vengeful. A chill clings to it, and the air grows still in its presence, as if sound itself recoils from the dead. |
