## Notes

### Strength Drain Note
**Weakening Touch** reduces the target’s Strength by **1d3** for **1 hour**.
- if this reduces the target’s Strength to **0**, the target dies

### Undead Note
Gloamshadows drift toward heat, light, and living breath, dimming their surroundings as they close.
