## Notes

### Boss Note
As a **Boss Creature**, Griselda always uses maximum hit points, for a total of **44 HP**.

### Summon Note
**Summon Boggart** is a bonus action with **Recharge 5/6**.
- roll **1d6** to determine the summoned creature
- the creature appears in a vacant square on the combat grid
- if there is no room, the action cannot be used
- results: **1** Boggart Knifer, **2** Rotwing, **3** Boggart Gasser, **4** Boggart Bruiser, **5-6** Boggart Rotwife
