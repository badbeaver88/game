# Umberhold Item List

This is a growing master item list for Umberhold.

It can include:
- mundane items
- magical items
- unique drops
- quest items
- consumables
- armor and weapons

It does **not** modify `MASTER.md`.

---

## Notes

- Settlement shop stock, hurled-item rules, and free class starting gear are tracked in `umberhold/mundane-equipment-canon.md`.
- Food and arrows are **not** tracked.
