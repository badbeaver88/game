# Crusader

## Class Summary

The **Crusader** is a heavily armed divine warrior who stands in the front rank, shields allies with sanctified presence, and strikes down evil with holy force.

### Core Identity
- **Primary Attributes:** Strength / Piety
- **Level 10 Title:** Divine Exemplar
- **Hit Die:** d10
- **Armor:** Any armor
- **Shield:** Can equip shield
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** All weapons
- **Spellcasting:** Half caster using **Piety**
- **Special Role:** Front-rank holy bruiser, anti-evil enforcer, defensive aura support, secondary healer

## Features

### Spellcasting
You can cast spells as a **half caster**.

Rules:
- the Crusader uses **Piety** as its spellcasting attribute
- spell access and spell recovery follow Umberhold's current spellcasting rules
- at character creation, a Crusader gains all currently approved **C 1st-level spells**
- the Crusader currently has **no approved cantrips**

---

### Sacred Touch
You may touch yourself or an ally to restore health equal to **1d8 per Crusader level**.

Rules:
- this ability recharges after a **Rest**
- you begin with **1 charge**
- at **Level 5**, this increases to **2 charges**
- at **Level 10**, this increases to **3 charges**

---

### Aura of Solace
Allies adjacent to you gain:
- **+1 to saving throws**
- **+1 AC** against attacks and spells from **evil creatures**

---

### Fearless Faith
You have **advantage** on all effects that would cause the **Frightened** condition.

---

### Plague Ward
You have **resistance** to all effects that cause disease.

---

### Uncapped Attribute Growth
You can increase:
- one attribute by **2**
- or two attributes by **1** each

---

### Holy Smite
Your melee attacks deal **one additional damage die** as **holy** damage when striking **evil creatures**.

---

### Blinding Judgement
When you hit an **evil creature** with a melee attack, it must succeed on a **Piety saving throw** against your **spell DC** or be **Blinded** until the end of its next turn.

---

### Exile the Unclean
When you strike a **fiend** or **undead** with a melee attack, it must succeed on a **Piety saving throw** against your **spell DC** or be **banished from the plane**.

Rules:
- your party gains **full experience** for defeating enemies this way
- **boss-type enemies are immune** to this effect

---

### Bulwark of Faith
While you have a **shield** equipped, attacks against you **cannot be critical hits**.

---

### Unshaken
You are **immune** to the **Frightened** condition.

---

### Purity Incarnate
You are **immune** to disease.

---

### Cleansing Touch
You may touch an ally to remove **all diseases** affecting them.

Rules:
- this ability recharges after a **Rest**

---

## Notes

### Current Role
The Crusader is currently defined as:
- front-rank half caster
- armored anti-evil melee combatant
- defensive support aura class
- secondary healer and cleanser
- strong fiend and undead counter-class

### Current Spell Access
Under current Umberhold spellcasting canon, the Crusader begins with:
- no approved cantrips
- all approved **C 1st-level spells**

The current early spell list includes:
- **Cure Minor Wounds**
- **Judgment of Solace**
- **Litany of Solace**
- **Shield of Solace**
- **Solace's Blessing**

### Current Open Mechanical Questions
These may need later clarification:
- how **adjacent** is defined for **Aura of Solace** in Umberhold's fixed-rank combat model
- how **evil creatures** are tagged and surfaced in combat UI for **Aura of Solace**, **Holy Smite**, and **Blinding Judgement**
- whether **Plague Ward**, **Purity Incarnate**, and **Cleansing Touch** interact with a future dedicated disease subsystem or current status-condition rules only
- whether **Exile the Unclean** may trigger from every qualifying melee hit without any per-turn or per-rest limit beyond landing the attack
