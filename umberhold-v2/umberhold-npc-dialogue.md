# Umberhold NPC Dialogue Matrix

This file contains the authored dialogue state changes for Umberhold's core NPCs.

Use these lines as conditional dialogue keyed off quest completion flags.

---

## 1. Bartholomew

### Default
**Greeting**  
> “Keep your voice low… walls carry sound these days. You here to stay, or just passing through… if passing through’s still a thing?”

**Village**  
> “Used to be folk laughed here. Now they listen. Always listening.”

**Manor**  
> “No one sane goes there by choice. Which means you’re either brave, desperate… or not from here.”

### After The Missing Alchemist
> “Word travels fast. Etheldreda’s back, and that’s more good news than we’ve had in months.”

> “Folk are speaking above a whisper again. Not many, but some.”

### After The Restless Dead
> “Heard bells at the graveyard this morning. Proper mourning bells. Haven’t heard that in too long.”

### After Troll Trouble
> “Water’s flowing and the buckets are filling. Strange how hopeful a village can sound when it stops dying of thirst.”

### After all three village quests
> “Still wouldn’t call this place safe. But for the first time in a while, I’d call it living.”

---

## 2. Oswaldine

### Default
**Greeting**  
> “Gold talks. If you’ve got it, I’ve got something worth your trouble.”

**Well warning**  
> “If you’re headed down there, buy oil. And if you’re clever, fire.”

**Graveyard warning**  
> “Dead things don’t stay buried east of here. Not lately.”

### After The Missing Alchemist
> “Good. The alchemist’s alive. Means I can stop hearing villagers ask me for cures I don’t stock.”

### After The Restless Dead
> “East side’s calmed down some. Funeral cloth and candle wax are selling again. Grim, but better than panic.”

### After Troll Trouble
> “Water trade’s back. Animals are drinking. People are washing. Turns out civilization matters.”

> “You did good work down there. Nasty work, but good.”

### After all three village quests
> “Village still limps. But it’s limping forward now, not toward the grave.”

---

## 3. Acolyte Gidney

### Default
**Greeting**  
> “Solace watches, even here… though her light feels distant.”

**Village**  
> “Something festers beneath this place. I feel it in my prayers.”

**Graveyard**  
> “The earth there is troubled. The dead are not at rest.”

### After The Missing Alchemist
> “You have broken one chain, though not the hand that forged them all. Still… one life returned is no small mercy.”

### After The Restless Dead
> “The graveyard is quieter now. Not silent—never silent—but no longer pleading.”

> “I prayed there at dawn. For the first time in many days, the prayer felt heard.”

### After Troll Trouble
> “Clean water is a blessing most only notice when it is threatened. The village owes you more than coin.”

### After all three village quests
> “The darkness around Umberhold has thinned, but it has not withdrawn. Do not mistake relief for victory.”

---

## 4. Wymarc

### Default
**Greeting**  
> “Ah—new faces. Good. Means you’ve not been scared off yet… or eaten.”

**Threat ranking**  
> “Manor’s the heart of it, I think. Graveyard’s uglier. Well’s deadlier. If I were you, I’d choose my battles careful.”

### After The Missing Alchemist
> “You brought her back. I’ll not pretend I expected that. Folk needed this win.”

> “Maybe now they’ll stop looking at me like I buried her myself.”

### After The Restless Dead
> “East side can bury their dead again. That matters. More than outsiders tend to think.”

### After Troll Trouble
> “Water’s ours again. Means no more pigs down a hole, and no more pretending tribute is strategy.”

### After all three village quests
> “You’ve done more for Umberhold than any writ, order, or warning I’ve given in months. I won’t forget it.”

---

## 5. Warden Halvric

### Default
**Greeting**  
> “Not safe beyond the walls. Not anymore.”

**Road warning**  
> “Road south looks open, if you don’t know better. But folk who try it come back wild-eyed—or don’t come back at all.”

### After The Missing Alchemist / Manor Boss Defeated
> “Orders changed. About time.”

> “If you’re going beyond the gates now, go armed and go ready. Whatever’s out there hasn’t stopped watching.”

### After The Restless Dead
> “Heard the graveyard settled. Good. Men sleep poorly when the dead start ideas.”

### After Troll Trouble
> “Water’s safe, they say. Good. A village without water isn’t a village for long.”

---

## 6. Etheldreda

### Post-rescue introduction
**Greeting**  
> “You have my thanks… though I confess I’d have preferred to meet you under less infernal circumstances.”

**About her capture**  
> “They dragged me below and made me brew. Not for healing. For vigor, filth, and violence.”

**About Wymarc’s closure note**  
> “Wymarc did what frightened men do—he covered the wound and prayed no one looked too close. I can hardly call him wrong. Only desperate.”

**About the village**  
> “Umberhold survives by habit. You’ve given it a chance to survive by hope.”

### After The Restless Dead
> “I can smell grave-soil less strongly on the wind now. That alone tells me you succeeded.”

### After Troll Trouble
> “Clean water improves every brew I make. More importantly, it improves every villager still breathing.”

### After all three village quests
> “Perhaps I shall hang a proper sign again. ‘Open,’ maybe. Imagine the audacity.”
